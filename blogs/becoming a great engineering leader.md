Investing in yourself is a great investment anytime! This is a personally curated list of over 200 resources to level up and grow into a great engineering leader in 2025.

## How to Start?

- Don’t try to learn all resources at once.
- Pick a few from categories you need most.
- Do a retrospective of your current skill set.
- Define where you need to improve.
